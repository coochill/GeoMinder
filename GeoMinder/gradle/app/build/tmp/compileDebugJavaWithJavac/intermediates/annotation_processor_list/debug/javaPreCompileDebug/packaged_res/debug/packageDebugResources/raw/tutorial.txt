##How to Use GeoMinder##




**Getting Started**




*Create Your First Task*
Tap "+" button on the dashboard.
Set a title and choose a category of the location such as ATM, coffee, shopping mall and etc.
Select a location by clicking a marker on the map or search for easy navigation.




*Customize Your Alerts*
Choose to get notified based on location proximity or time triggers.




*Stay Organized*
View all tasks on your categorized list.
Easily edit, complete, or delete tasks as needed.
